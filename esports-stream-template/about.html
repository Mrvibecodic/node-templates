About

One arena that never goes dark

ArenaCast is a 24/7 esports broadcast hub. We pull the official live channels from the world's biggest tournament operators into a single place — so there is always a match, a rerun or the next region on screen.

What ArenaCast is

We don't produce broadcasts ourselves. ArenaCast is a viewing layer: a curated grid of the official 24/7 channels run by operators like ESL, PGL and Riot Games, plus a unified schedule that spans every title we cover — Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2, League of Legends and StarCraft II.

The goal is simple: remove the tab-juggling. Instead of hunting across platforms for what's live right now, you open one page and pick a title.

How it works

01

We aggregate

Official live and rerun channels from trusted tournament operators are organised by game into a single grid.

02

You pick a title

Choose CS2, Dota 2, LoL or StarCraft II. Players load on demand so the page stays fast.

03

Never miss a match

A merged schedule across every title lets you filter by game and set reminders for upcoming matches.

Rights & attribution

All broadcasts are streamed via Twitch and belong to their respective operators. ArenaCast is an independent aggregator and is not affiliated with Twitch or the listed studios. If you operate a tournament and would like your channel added or removed, head to our partners page or get in touch.
