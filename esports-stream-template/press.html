Boilerplate

ArenaCast is a 24/7 esports broadcast hub that aggregates the official live and rerun channels of the world's biggest tournament operators into one place, alongside a unified, filterable schedule across Counter-Strike 2, Dota 2, League of Legends and StarCraft II.

Fast facts

Format

Aggregator

A viewing layer over official operator channels — we don't produce broadcasts ourselves.

Titles

Four games

CS2, Dota 2, League of Legends and StarCraft II, with more in rotation over time.

Access

Invite-only

Membership is currently invite-based; public sign-ups are temporarily closed.

Logo & brand

The ArenaCast wordmark and logo are available on request. Please don't alter the proportions or colours. Game titles, operator names and their logos belong to their respective owners and are referenced for identification only.
